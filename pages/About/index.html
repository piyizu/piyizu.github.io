About Me

I am Piyi Zu, a PhD student at National University of Defense Technology. I have got a bachelor's degree in computer science and technology from Xiamen University. I am currently interested in program analysis, especially static analysis and symbolic execution. If you have any ideas or you are also interested in those fields, do not hesitate to contact me. We can co-operate, share ideas and help each other. You can find my email address on the left navigation panel of the website.
